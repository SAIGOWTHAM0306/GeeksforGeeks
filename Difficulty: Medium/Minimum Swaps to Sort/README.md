<strong style="user-select: auto;">Explanation: </strong>swap 8 with 4.</span></pre>
<pre style="user-select: auto;"><span style="font-size: 14pt; user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Input: </strong>arr[] = [10, 19, 6, 3, 5]
<strong style="user-select: auto;">Output: </strong>2
<strong style="user-select: auto;">Explanation: </strong>swap 10 with 3 and swap 19 with 5.<br style="user-select: auto;"></span></pre>
